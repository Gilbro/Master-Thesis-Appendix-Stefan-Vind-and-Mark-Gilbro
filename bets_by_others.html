Here you can see all games that are ready for you to bet on.

To start betting you can*:

• Choose a game to bet on and create your own bet.

• Select a game to see all bets created on the game.

• Select from the most recently created bets if you see something interesting.

• All created bets that are not taken by and opponent will be cancelled and you will get all ethers back to your wallet.

*All payouts will be deducted a 2% fee for maintaining the system
